Distributed services for international clients

Over the past few years, I created an online service business. The initial task was to truly understand the, often unspoken, needs of primarily US clients. Once, the needs had been understood the main objective was to up sell them to other services. As an example a customer who wanted a $100 business card ended up spending $1,200 on a website. The work was then outsourced to foreign workers resulting in up to 95% net profit margins. Thousands of pounds of net profit are a testimony for how I can convince customers to purchase. Excellent sales strategies were critical to the success of these projects.

Specific projects included:

- VFX work for a short film

- Editing several videos, shown to 15,000 people

- Creating a comic book for an escape room

- CAD drawings for architects

- Website for an interior design company

I learnt a lot about how to approach a purchase of high value. Furthermore, the experience I gained in negotiating have proved crucial in other jobs, such as the distribution of online services.
